Our story

We believe every journey
should feel safe, fair and dignified.

Bica Driver was born in Lagos from a simple frustration: trusting a stranger with your car shouldn't be a gamble, and skilled drivers shouldn't struggle to find honest work. We built a marketplace that respects both sides of the wheel.

Our mission

Connecting trust, one ride at a time.

For car owners, we remove the anxiety of handing over the keys. Every driver is identity verified, background checked and continuously rated. For drivers, we open the door to steady, well-paid, dignified work with clients who value their craft.

It's a marketplace built on one belief: when both sides win, everybody rides better.

"Connect. Drive. Thrive."

Three words that shape every decision we make, from how we vet a driver to how we price a trip.

What we stand for

The values behind the wheel.

Safety first, always

We screen harder than anyone in the market because trust can never be an afterthought.

Fairness for drivers

Drivers keep the majority of every fare and get paid on time, every time. Dignity is non-negotiable.

Community over transactions

Ratings, badges and loyal clients turn one off rides into lasting relationships and real careers.
